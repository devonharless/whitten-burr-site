Whitten & Burr

We are a UX consultancy group. We help startups in the tech industry create clear and profound interactive experiences.

  • Our Passion

    We care about seamless interactive experiences that are accessible from the desktop or your mobile browser. We care about the usability of your information and website. We revel in the details. We love to ask, "Why?" And we enjoy a good challenge.

  • Our Services

    We design. We build. We test. We ask questions and we problem-solve. We offer user experience and interactive design services for startups in the mobile/web business. We have the tools, experience, and know-how to take a project from an idea to reality.

Services

  • Information Architecture

    content inventory, sitemaps, making sense of data

    We look at the content you already have, identify holes, suggest updates, and reorganize your data to be more impactful.

  • Interaction Design

    personas, use cases, user stories, wireframes

    We create guidelines and plans to better enable your users while using your app.

  • Usability Engineering

    html/css/jquery prototypes, usability testing, user research

    High fidelity prototypes and user testing help to hone and fine-tune your app to find weak links.

  • Web Standards

    design patterns, accessibility, style tiles

    We define design rules that are easy for developers to implement and users to understand.
